About

"VERIVERY" (Hangul: 베리베리) is a South Korean boy group under Jellyfish Entertainment. The group consist of seven members: Dongheon, Hoyoung, Minchan, Gyehyeon, Yeonho, Yongseung and Kangmin. They debuted with their 1st mini album (EP) "VERI-US" on January 9, 2019.
